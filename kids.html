Program

Interactive Exhibits:

Students can engage with over 1,000 exhibits on topics like robotics, energy, dinosaurs, and space exploration. Each exhibit is designed to spark curiosity, encourage critical thinking, and provide hands-on experiences that make science come alive.

Tailored Programs:

Choose from educational workshops and guided tours designed to match your curriculum. Our expert educators ensure each program is interactive, engaging, and tailored to meet the needs of diverse learning styles and grade levels.

Exclusive Teacher Resources:

Download pre-visit materials, activity sheets, and post-visit challenges to enrich the learning experience. These resources are carefully crafted to help you extend the impact of your visit and seamlessly integrate museum learning into your classroom activities.

Practical information

We offer a variety of pre-planned trips designed to maximize learning and engagement for students.

tailored resources to enhance the visit and make planning a breeze.
