Museum Info

Join us at the Community Science Museum, where science comes alive for everyone! From the elements of fire, water, wind, and earth to the mysteries of galaxies and life itself, our exhibits showcase the wonders of discovery. With over 1,000 exhibits—many interactive—you can explore, learn, and ignite a lifelong passion for science. (Just don't expect the dinosaurs to move during the day—they wait until we're gone!)

Museum Café

Recharge at our cozy café with light lunches like sandwiches, salads, and soups,
plus vegetarian, gluten-free, and allergy-friendly options. Enjoy coffee, tea, soft drinks,
and delicious snacks to fuel your visit!

Accessibility

The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps. It also has audio guides and braille
display signs for the visually impaired.

Ticket information

Tickets can be purchased easily online or at the reception.
Your ticket provides full access to the museum's fascinating exhibits and experiences.

Don't miss these unique experiences—plan your visit
to catch them on their scheduled days!
