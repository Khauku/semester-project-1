DinoVerse

DinoVerse: Bringing Dinosaurs to Life

Step back in time and explore the incredible world of dinosaurs at DinoVerse! This immersive exhibit brings the prehistoric era to life with fascinating displays and interactive experiences, and life-sized dinosaur recreations. See T-Rex, uncover hidden fossils, and dive into the mysteries of a time when giants roamed the Earth. Perfect for adventurers of all ages, DinoVerse offers a journey millions of years old.

Exhibits at DinoVerse

Fossil Dig Zone:

Channel your inner paleontologist and uncover hidden fossils in our
excavation pit. Experience the thrill
of discovery as you dig, sift, just like
a real paleontologist!

Dinosaur Sounds:

Hear what dinosaurs may have sounded like through cutting-
edge sound reconstructions.
Immerse yourself in their world by
experiencing the roars.

Dino Anatomy:

Explore the biology of dinosaurs with interactive models showing
how these ancient creatures lived,
moved, and hunted.

Virtual Dino Safari:

Use VR technology to walk
alongside dinosaurs in their natural
habitats and witness their world like
never before.
